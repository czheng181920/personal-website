Pokémusic

Explore the creative intersection of technology and entertainment with my latest project, Pokémusic. This innovative website, built with Next.js and Redux, leverages the power of OpenAI and Spotify to predict a Pokémon's favorite music genre and retrieve song data.

Key Highlights:

  • Utilized Next.js to optimize image load time, ensuring a seamless and fast user experience.
  • Employed OpenAI to generate fun and accurate music predictions for different Pokémon.
  • Integrated Spotify Web API to provide users with real-time song data, enhancing the interactive experience.

MedDevice Search

MedDevice, a project that received the Social Impact Award at the Upenn Femmehacks Hackathon, aims to demystify the FDA clearance process for medical devices. Built with HTML, CSS, Node.js, and Figma, this impactful application empowers patients and helps open up the black box that is the medical device industry.

Key Highlights:

  • MedDevice enables users to easily check and understand the FDA clearance process for their medical devices, promoting transparency and informed decision-making.
  • The platform connects patients to the FDA's official reporting mechanism and online communities of individuals facing similar challenges, fostering support and shared experiences.
