3-D Choropleth Map • 2021 Research Internship
PAPER
This data visualization project uses R to model cancer disparities as 3-D, interactive graphs. Work was done in CWRU's Department of Population and Quantitative Health Sciences. ...

Unlike other data visualization tools like ArcGis, using R enables researchers to easily document their code and conduct reproducible research. Data was sourced from the Ohio Department of Health and the United States Cancer Statistics to contrast sex-specific colorectal cancer incidence rates across Ohio counties.

My contributions: All graphs, analysis, and research paper (click button below the gif to view!)
